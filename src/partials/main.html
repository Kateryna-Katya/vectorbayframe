Vector Bay

Draw your route, deliver the cargo.

Vector Bay is a dockside puzzle game where every delivery depends on the path you draw.

Chart Your Course

How It Works

  • Survey the Bay

    Each level shows you a harbor grid — cargo frames waiting at the dock, bays marked by destination color.

  • Draw Your Vectors

    Tap and drag to place directional arrows across the grid, routing each container along the most efficient path.

  • Beat the Tide

    Once your route is set, release the cargo and watch it travel — adjust before the water rises and the bay floods.

Features

  • Blueprint Puzzles

    Every level's handcrafted. We don't use any random generators or anything like that. Each puzzle has that perfect solution just waiting for you to discover it.

  • Vector Chaining

    Chain arrows together to split cargo or change direction halfway through, and solve multi-destination deliveries in a single move.

  • Rising Tide Pressure

    The tide's always rising, but don't stress — we won't punish you for taking your time to think things through.

  • Unlockable Districts

    Progress through five distinct harbor zones, each introducing new mechanics like cranes, one-way currents, and locked bay gates.
