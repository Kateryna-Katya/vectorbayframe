Vector Bay

Draw your route, deliver the cargo.

Vector Bay is a dockside puzzle game where every delivery depends on the path you draw.

Chart Your Course

How It Works

  • Survey the Bay

    Each level shows you a harbor grid — cargo frames waiting at the dock, bays marked by destination color.

  • Draw Your Vectors

    Tap and drag to place directional arrows across the grid, routing each container along the most efficient path.

  • Beat the Tide

    Once your route is set, release the cargo and watch it travel — adjust before the water rises and the bay floods.
